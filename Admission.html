Admission Process

Admission Process for 2021-2022 has started, Last date for applying is 28 March. Apply now!

1)We follow guideline given by Education Board of Singapore for Admissions in all the courses.
2)As per the rules from total seats 75% seats are filled by central admission committee, remaining 25% seats are kept for foreign students in all the courses and admission is done according to the rules given board of education.
3)Those foreign students who are interested for securing admission are required to contact institute directly.
4)All the decisions regarding admission will be taken by the university and the decision of the university would be final.
5)Please refer the following table for getting a brief idea about the seats allocation.
6)Those foreign students are requested to read all the process and rules of admission given by the board of education.
